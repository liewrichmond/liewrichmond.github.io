About Me

I’m a computer engineering student in Northeastern University’s graduating class of 2020 looking to make a difference with my degree. With computer engineering having such a wide spectrum of applications, I hope to expose myself to as many sectors as possible before embedding myself deep in a particular field.

At the time of writing, I’ve had experience working within the medical technology industry, with my most recent internship being at Micro-Leads Inc. During my time there, I worked in a cross-functional, interdisciplinary team on research and development of their products.

I pride myself in being a generalist and I believe that there’s no task too small. Time and time again, I have put myself in the position to undertake different tasks, this has honed in on my problem solving skills and I aim to provide creative software based solutions for your problems.
