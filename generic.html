Capstone Project 2019

For my senior capstone project, my team and I have decided to focus on Robot Navigation with the use case of search and rescue in mind. With the resources provided to us by Northeasternâ€™s Sica-lab, we were able to fully integrate existing libraries into our project to create a mesh connected autonomous mapping robot swarm.

The project leveraged Robot Operating System as a framework for our robots. The main hardware platform used was the Turtlebot3 and the ad hoc routing protocol used was B.A.T.M.A.N Adv.

As seen in the demo above, we have successfully implemented multi-agent exploration of robots that create a single merged map. The success of the project has led to myself being employed by the Sica-Lab in Spring 2020 in order to further refine the project and integrate the technology into their current research.

Due to the sponsorship provided by the Sica-Lab, the implementation details are proprietary and cannot be disclosed without proper approval.
